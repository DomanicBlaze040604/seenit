## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. Go to the **SeenIt** homepage
2. Click **Sign Up** in the top-right corner
3. Enter your email and create a password
4. Check your inbox for a confirmation link and click it
5. You're in! You'll be redirected to your **Dashboard**

### Signing In

1. Click **Login** from the homepage
2. Enter your registered email and password
3. Click **Sign In** — you'll land on your Dashboard

## Your Dashboard

The Dashboard is your home base. Here's what you'll see:

### Stats at a Glance
- **Total Reviews** — How many reviews you've uploaded
- **Approved** — Reviews that passed SARS™ verification
- **Watch Time** — Total duration of all your review videos
- **Trust Level** — Your current tier: `NEW`, `TRUSTED`, or `ELITE`

### SARS™ Score
Your SARS™ (System for Authentic Review Scoring) score is displayed as a circular gauge out of 100. It's calculated from four factors:

| Factor | Weight | What It Measures |
|--------|--------|------------------|
| **Experience Validity** | 40% | Real usage, logical timing, depth |
| **Reviewer Credibility** | 30% | Consistency, accuracy, bias |
| **Content Quality** | 20% | Specificity, usefulness, depth |
| **Engagement Integrity** | 10% | Organic patterns, time signals |

> **Tip:** Upload more reviews with proof (receipts, unboxing videos) to raise your score.

### Recent Reviews
A quick list of your latest submissions with their status:
- 🟢 **Approved** — Live and visible to viewers
- 🟡 **Pending** — Under SARS™ review
- 🔴 **Rejected** — Did not pass verification

## Uploading a Review

Navigate to **Upload Review** from the sidebar or dashboard button.

### Step 1: Upload Video
- Drag and drop your video file, or click **Browse Files**
- Supported formats: MP4, MOV, WebM
- Maximum size: 500MB
- Record your honest experience — no scripts, no staging

### Step 2: Select Product
- Choose the product you're reviewing from the list
- Products are organized by name and category
- If the product isn't listed, you can skip this step

### Step 3: Review Details
- **Title** — Give your review a clear, descriptive title (e.g., "6 months with AirPods Pro — honest take")
- **Proof Type** — Select what kind of evidence you have:
  - 🕐 **Daily Usage** — You use this product regularly
  - 🧾 **Purchased** — You bought it yourself (receipt available)
  - 📦 **Unboxing** — First-time opening/setup
  - ⚖️ **Comparison** — Side-by-side with another product
- **Months of Usage** — How long you've used the product

### Step 4: Submit
- Review your details on the summary screen
- Click **Submit Review**
- Your review will enter the SARS™ verification pipeline
- You'll see it in **My Reviews** with a "Pending" status

## How SARS™ Works

SARS™ (System for Authentic Review Scoring) is SeenIt's trust engine. Every review is scored across five dimensions:

1. **Experience Validity** — Was the product actually used? For how long?
2. **Reviewer Credibility** — Is the reviewer consistent and unbiased?
3. **Content Quality** — Is the review specific, useful, and well-structured?
4. **Engagement Integrity** — Are interactions organic, not manipulated?
5. **Outcome Impact** — Does the review help others make decisions?

The resulting **Dynamic Authenticity Score** determines:
- Whether the review is published
- How prominently it appears in search and feeds
- Whether the reviewer earns from their content

## Tips for Great Reviews

1. ✅ **Be honest** — Share what worked AND what didn't
2. ✅ **Show proof** — Receipts, packaging, or usage footage
3. ✅ **Be specific** — "Battery lasts 38 hours" beats "great battery"
4. ✅ **Film naturally** — No scripts, no staging, just your experience
5. ✅ **Include context** — How long have you used it? Would you buy again?
6. ❌ **Don't fake it** — SARS™ will catch it and lower your score
7. ❌ **Don't copy** — Original content only
